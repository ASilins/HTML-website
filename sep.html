SEP

Our goal is to make our students ready for their jobs. In a professional environment, most work is done in teams. In the SEP course students work in groups which they get to choose at the beginning of the semester. After forming, these groups are assigned with project. Most of the work is done outside of SEP classes though. The course's main focus is to teach the students to work in teams.

SEP

At VIA many of the courses are based on group work. From the first week you will join a study group, in which you have the opportunity to discuss, ask questions, do exercises, and find solutions to various issues. Many students also use the groups for course or exam preparation.

Why group work?

When you work in a group, you get the opportunity to develop multiple perspectives on the issues you study. This type of synergy will often be advantageous to the project, because your fellow students may draw your attention to issues you were unaware of. Joining forces with other students in projects and solutions of various tasks may stimulate your creativity, generate more ideas, improve your rhetoric, and in the final analysis enhance your product. The group work mode also gives you the opportunity to solve complex tasks with greater efficiency than if you were on your own.
